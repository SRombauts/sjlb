Typographic Scale :
12sp=Text Size Micro
14sp=Text Size Small
18sp=Text Size Medium
22sp=Text Size Large


Metrics :
48dp=Icon(border included),Action Bar...
40dp=Button
32dp=Icon(center part, border excluded)
16dp=Lateral spacing
8dp=Icon border, spacing
4dp=Margin arround button
